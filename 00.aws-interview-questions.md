# 1. AWS VPC Troubleshooting Interview Questions

These questions focus on networking issues in **Amazon Virtual Private Cloud**.

1. An EC2 instance in a **public subnet cannot access the internet**. What will you check?

2. Your EC2 instance is in a **private subnet and cannot download updates**. How will you troubleshoot?

3. Your instance cannot reach the internet even though **Internet Gateway** is attached. What else could be wrong?

4. Two EC2 instances in the **same VPC cannot communicate**. What could cause this?

5. Your application server in a **private subnet cannot access the database server in another subnet**. What will you check?

6. You created a VPC but **instances cannot access the internet**. What configuration might be missing?

7. Your EC2 instance is reachable via **private IP but not public IP**. What could be the issue?

8. You allowed port 22 in the **Security Group**, but SSH still fails. What else should you verify?

9. A **route table is configured but traffic is not flowing**. What could be wrong?

10. Your instance has a **public IP but cannot reach external websites**. Why?

11. Your EC2 instance cannot connect to another EC2 instance in a **different subnet in the same VPC**.

12. You accidentally **deleted a route in the route table**. What will happen?

13. Your application is accessible internally but **not from the internet**.

14. Your EC2 instance needs **outbound internet but should not allow inbound traffic**. How will you design the network?

15. Your EC2 instance is in a **private subnet but must access the internet**. Which AWS component will you use?
